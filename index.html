About the Game

Garden Block Quest is a single-player sliding block puzzle built around clear rules and thoughtful layouts. Each board asks you to study the space, move pieces in the right order, and open a route to the exit.

The game is designed for quick breaks and deeper puzzle sessions alike. Smooth touch controls, readable block colors, move tracking, and star goals make every solved level feel rewarding without adding pressure.

Support

If you need help, have a question, or want to report an issue, please use the support information below before submitting your request through the app's App Store product page.

Common Questions

  • Progress is missing: Progress is stored locally on your iPhone. Deleting the app may remove local progress.
  • Sound or haptics are not working: Open Settings in the app and confirm Sound and Haptics are enabled. Also check your device volume and silent mode.
  • A puzzle feels stuck: Use Undo to step back one move, or Reset to restart the current puzzle.
  • Display issue: Use a supported iPhone running iOS 13.0 or later and update to the latest App Store version.

Request Support

To request support without sharing personal information on this website, open the app's App Store product page and use the available App Store support or review channel with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.
