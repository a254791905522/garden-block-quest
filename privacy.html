Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Data Collection

Garden Block Quest does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal information.

Garden Block Quest is an offline, single-player puzzle game. It does not require an account, login, internet connection, or registration.

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No device identifiers are collected.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No information is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

Local Game Data

Game progress, best move counts, star ratings, and sound or haptic settings are stored locally on your iPhone only. This local data stays on your device and is protected by iOS device-level security.

If you delete the app, the locally stored game progress may also be removed from your device.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate third-party services, including:

  • No advertising SDKs and no ads.
  • No analytics frameworks.
  • No social media integrations.
  • No in-app purchase systems.
  • No external crash reporting or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Garden Block Quest is suitable for ages 4+. Because the app does not collect any personal information from anyone, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

No personal data is transmitted to servers because the app does not collect personal data. Local progress remains on the device and is subject to the user's normal iOS device settings, backups, and deletion choices.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the app changes. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or need support, please use the app's App Store product page support or review channel.
